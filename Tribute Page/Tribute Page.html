Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

The Last Prophet and Messenger

Here's a time line of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ life

  • Makkah Phase
  • 570 AD - Birth of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ in Makkah
  • 610 AD - Granted Prophethood at 40 years old and received the first revelation
  • 622 AD - Prophet's migration from Makkah to Madinah
  • Madinah Phase
  • 624 AD - The Day of Al-Furqan / The Battle of Badr
  • 630 AD - Qonquest of Makkah
  • 632 AD - Prophet's Hajj of Farewell
  • 633 AD - Passing away of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ in Madinah
